Handmade Ceramics
and Pottery

The forming of vessels and other objects with clay and other ceramic materials.
Pottery is one of the oldest human inventions, originating before the Neolithic period.

The process of making pottery includes shaping the clay, trimming, glazing and then firing.

First the clay is shaped by
hand or on the throwing wheel.

Next the piece is trimmed
using various trimming tools.

The piece is then glazed.

Lastly the work is fired in a kiln.
